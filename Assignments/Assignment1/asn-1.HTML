Tourist Places In Salem



Title: Yercaud

Description:

Yercaud is a hill station in the Eastern Ghats of Tamil Nadu, situated at an altitude of 1,515 meters (4,970 feet) in the Shevaroy Hills, known as the 'Ooty of the Poor

Title: Mettur dam

Description:

Mettur Dam, located in Salem District, Tamil Nadu, is a masonry gravity dam built across the Kaveri River in 1934, designed by engineer W.M. Ellis and constructed over nine years with the labor of 17,000 men

Title: kurumbapatti zoo

Description:

Kurumbapatti Zoological Park, located in Salem, Tamil Nadu, is situated at the foothills of the Shervaroyan Hills and spans 76 acres of forest land, housing over 254 animals including deer, crocodiles, monkeys, and various bird species.
